What We Are Doing


Our organization has safe houses available for those who are feeling hopeless. We are available 24/7 to pick up anyone in need of assistance no matter where you are. We guarantee your anonymity and have any and all supplies available that may be needed in a crisis. We will always have someone onsite available to talk to who's been through it and can understand how you are feeling. You are not alone.



The semicolon is the symbol for Suicide Awareness. A semicolon is used when the author could have ended a sentence but chose to keep it going. Your story isn't over yet; and we're helping you tell the world you're story. Every Saturday we have meetings for survivors to speak out about their battle. We also offer the option for you to write your story for us to publish. We are collecting stories for a book we would like for the world to read in hopes to inspire those who are lost and save someones life.

You have a friend for life once you reach out to us. We are available 24/7 through phone, email, or live chat. We will provide you with a genuine heartfelt conversation for as much time as you need. You're life is our number one priority and we will never give up making sure you know how worthy of life you are.
